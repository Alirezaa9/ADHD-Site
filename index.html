Why you need to learn about ADHD

Close to 10% of the population has ADHD

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects approximately 10% of people worldwide. It is characterized by difficulties with attention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity.

Something else about ADHD ####

Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental disorder that affects approximately 10% of people worldwide. It is characterized by difficulties with attention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity.

It's not easily diagnosed

Diagnosing ADHD can be challenging due to the variability in symptoms and the overlap with other conditions. It often requires comprehensive evaluation by a qualified healthcare professional, considering various factors such as medical history, behavioral observations, and psychological assessments.

It's easily misdiagnosed with other disorders

ADHD can be mistaken for other mental health disorders such as anxiety, depression, or learning disabilities. Misdiagnosis can occur when symptoms are not properly assessed or when there is a lack of understanding about ADHD's diverse presentations. Accurate diagnosis is crucial for appropriate treatment and support.
